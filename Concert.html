CONCENTUS MUSICORUM FINIS

The pursuit for the end is one destined to be trapped in an amphitheater of apocolyptic ruptures. The end of a physical entity is reaching the inevitable breakpoint of fractalization, yet the end of a melody is not its goal: but nonetheless, had the melody not reached its end it would not have reached its goal either. You are invited on this jouurney to bear witness the manifestation of such melodies: Welcome to the Concert of the end.
